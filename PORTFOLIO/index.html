About Me
Designing Is My Passion

a realm where creativity dances with logic, and pixels weave the fabric of user experiences. Let me share a snippet of my journey, where serendipity led me to this captivating world. As a child, I wielded pencils like magic wands, sketching whimsical worlds on paper. But as the years flowed, my artistic muse waned. Replicating scenes stroke by stroke no longer stirred my soul. Then, in the fall of 2018, I stumbled upon graphic design—a digital canvas that rekindled my creative fire. With Adobe Illustrator as my wand, I conjured layouts, logos, and visual narratives. The thrill of designing consumed me, much like those childhood hours spent drawing. I embraced design roles across campus clubs—magazines, newspapers, theater groups, and business clubs. Yet, amidst this whirlwind, I yearned for more. And then it happened—a pivotal project. A chance to shape a meaningful solution. The canvas expanded beyond aesthetics; it became a bridge between my scientific curiosity and my desire to solve real-world problems. UI/UX design emerged—the perfect intersection of my passions. It wasn’t accidental; it was destiny. So here I am, weaving pixels into journeys, sculpting interfaces that dance with users’ dreams. My passion? It’s etched in every wireframe, every color choice, and every delightful interaction. UI/UX design—it’s not just my path; it’s my compass guiding me toward the heart of human experiences1.
